Classic Pig
  • Once a 1 is rolled, your turn automatically ends and you get no points that round.
  • Rolling any other numbers gets added to your points for that turn.
  • "Holding" ends your turn. Points are then added to the total score.

Two-Dice Pig
  • If no dice show 1, you may add the two numbers to your current round points.
  • Upon rolling a 1, your turn ends and no points are earned.
  • When two 1s are rolled, your overall score is reset to 0.
