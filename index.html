Pig is a family dice game first mentioned by John Scarne in 1945.

A simple game with origins from the classic Pass the Pig, Pig is a game that's fast, easy, and has many variations. It is also popularly used by mathematics teachers to instill probability concepts. As a jeapordy dice game, decisions are made in game on whether to risk losing your gains to get ahead.
