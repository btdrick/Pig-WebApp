The Objective üíØ

The objective of Pig is to roll dice to be the first one to achieve 100 points. On each turn, players weigh decisions on maximizing potential point gains while avoiding rolling a "bad number" and losing points.
These are the traditional rules, but the amount of dice involved, points needed, and bad numbers cast are adjustable.

Yes, it's really that simple.
